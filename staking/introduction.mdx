## Welcome to Staking & Delegation

This section covers everything you need to know about staking and delegation in the easeflow ecosystem.

## What is Staking?

Staking is the process of participating in the network consensus mechanism by locking up your tokens to help secure the network and earn rewards.

## What is Delegation?

Delegation allows token holders to delegate their voting power and stake to validators while maintaining ownership of their tokens.
