## Welcome to floAgent

floAgent is an intelligent automation agent designed to streamline operations and enhance user experience within the easeflow ecosystem.

## What is floAgent?

floAgent is an AI-powered automation tool that helps users:
- Automate complex workflows
- Monitor network activities
- Execute smart contract interactions
- Optimize staking and delegation strategies
