## Your Node Companion, Right in Telegram

floAgent keeps you in the loop with **real-time alerts**, **insights**, and **platform updates**, delivered instantly through Telegram. Whether you're managing a single node or scaling across networks, floAgent ensures you stay informed, proactive, and always one step ahead.

<Note>
This is just the beginning! floAgent will evolve into your all-in-one operational sidekick, giving you everything you need to thrive in decentralized infrastructure.
</Note>
