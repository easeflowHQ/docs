## Welcome to control room of the new internet!
<Tip>
Tag along as we build the toolset for a decentralized sphere of **fair chances** and **equal opportunities**.
</Tip>

easeflow is your gateway to simplified blockchain infrastructure. Whether you're deploying nodes, staking tokens, or leveraging AI automation, we've got you covered.
Build, automate, and orchestrate decentralized infrastructure—without the complexity.

aseflow is your launchpad for spinning up powerful networks, coordinating participation, and activating utility across decentralized infrastructure.

Whether you're here to deploy your first node, automate smart workflows, or explore the architecture behind next-generation DePIN networks, you’re in the right place.

What you'll find here
This Learning Hub is designed to help you:

Understand the easeflow ecosystem

Set up your infrastructure with floClick

Automate workflows using floAgent

Get familiar with delegation, orchestration, and participation at scale

We’re keeping things simple, modular, and transparent—so you can focus on building, not troubleshooting.
