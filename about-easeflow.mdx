## What is easeflow?

easeflow is a comprehensive blockchain infrastructure platform that provides powerful tools and services for node deployment, staking, delegation, and AI-powered automation.

### Core Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="server" title="Node Deployment">
    Deploy and manage blockchain nodes with ease using our streamlined infrastructure. 
    Our platform handles the complexity of node setup, monitoring, and maintenance so you can 
    focus on building and growing your network presence.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="coins" title="Staking & Delegation">
    Participate in network consensus and earn rewards through our secure staking platform.
    Delegate your tokens to trusted validators or run your own validator node with 
    competitive rewards and industry-leading security practices.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="robot" title="floAgent AI Automation">
    Leverage AI-powered automation to optimize your blockchain operations.
    Our intelligent agent monitors network conditions, executes strategies, and 
    manages your assets 24/7 with advanced risk management protocols.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
