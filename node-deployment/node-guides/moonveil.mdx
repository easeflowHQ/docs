## Overview

Moonveil.gg is a rising zk-L2 & AI powered gaming ecosystem, built by a team of seasoned gaming and blockchain veterans previously at Riot Games, Tencent, Netease, and CARV. Focused on building the best web3 games, Moonveil has seen strong growth in the past 12 months, reaching over 1.68 million registered users. Of these, more than 900k are traditional gamers onboarded from web2 to web3 through our platform, making Moonveil one of the world's most active gaming communities.
