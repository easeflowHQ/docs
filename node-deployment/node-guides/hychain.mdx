## Overview

HYCHAIN is a blockchain (Ethereum L2 Rollup) and suite of web3 infrastructure that was created to eliminate onboarding and technical challenges for web3 games aiming for widespread adoption. With years of experience in building leading brands and companies within the industry, our team is dedicated to positioning HYCHAIN as the premier web3 gaming chain.
