## Overview

Circuit Nodes are the backbone of the GR1D Network, playing a critical role in transaction validation, cross-chain communication, scalability, and the execution of decentralized application logic. As GR1D aims to support a vast, decentralized Web3 ecosystem, the role of Circuit Nodes is essential in maintaining the network's integrity, scalability, and decentralized structure.
