## Overview

RCADE Network is a decentralized blockchain redefining gaming, media, and digital entertainment in alliance with Revolving Games. At the heart of the ecosystem are player-powered RCADE Nodes they are the foundation for a reliable, scalable infrastructure. In return, node operators are rewarded with significant $RCADE incentives, ensuring long-term engagement and network resilience. Start powering the network.
