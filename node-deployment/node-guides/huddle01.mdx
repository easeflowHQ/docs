## Overview

Huddle01 is building 1st DePIN dedicated to making real-time communication performant, secure and 95% cheaper. Share your unused Bandwidth and democratize audio/video connectivity with Huddle01. It is designed to provide ultra-low latency, enhanced data privacy and borderless audio/video connectivity across industries like AI, GameFi, Metaverse, SocialFi, etc.
